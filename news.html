ABOUT ANINDYO HOBBY CLUB

Anindya hobby club is a service club for young people ages 18 to old who are dedicated to finding innovative solutions to the world’s most pressing challenges while developing leadership skills and making friends from around the world.

Anindya hobby club decide how to organize and run their own clubs, manage their funds, and plan and carry out activities and service projects that are important to their communities.

Anindya hobby club that sponsor Rotaract clubs offer guidance and support and work with Anindya hobby club as partners in service.

Purpose

Students and young professionals to take action through community and international service that creates sustainable solutions to local and global challenges, connect with the global community of Rotary to expand professional networks, exchange ideas with leaders, and cultivate lifelong friendship around the world, develop skills to become community and global leaders, make friends locally and globally, and have fun while recognizing the importance of Service Above Self.

Mission & Vision

Anindya hobby club of Daffodil International University is established on June 23 in 2015, with the intention of proving students colossal platforms of extracurricular activities to acuminate their inner power.

The purpose of this club is to provide opportunities for young and women to enhance the knowledge and skills that will assist them in professional development and also in serving the community. By doing such career-related and community-based activities, the young minds will also be ensured of the networks between the professionals and them.

Here, our elementary motive is to do community services as well as to undertake self-developmental or professional projects, such as seminars, webinars, idea competitions, workshops, and so on. To ascertain club members' likewise youths' productivity to help them get to their ambitions and goals.
